Mojito Recipe

Description

A refreshing and popular Cuban cocktail, the Mojito combines white rum, fresh mint leaves, lime juice, simple syrup, and club soda. It's a delightful blend of sweet, minty, and citrusy flavors.

Ingredients

  • White rum - 60 ml
  • Freshly squeezed lime juice - 30 ml
  • 1 tablespoon (15ml) Simple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 6-8 Fresh mint leaves
  • Club soda (soda water) or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional:Lime wedges and additional mint springs for garnish

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Mint:
    • Gently slap the mint leaves between your palms or clap them once to release their aromatic oils. This enhances the minty flavour of the Mojito.
  2. Muddle the Mint:
    • Place the mint leaves at the botton of a sturdy glass or a cocktail shaker
    • Use a mudder(or the back of a spoon) to gently press and bruise the mint, releasing its flavours
  3. Add Lime Juice and Simple Syrup
    • Pour the freshly squeezed lime juice and simple syrup over the muddled mint in the glass or shaker
  4. Add White Rum:
    • Pour the white rum into the glass or shaker
  5. Shake (Optional):
    • If using a cocktail shaker, add some ice cubes and shake the mixture gently to combine the flavors. If you're using a glass, you can skip this step.
  6. Add ice:
    • Fill the glass with ice cubes
  7. Top with Club Soda:
    • Pour club soda or sparkling water over the ice to fill the glass
  8. Stir:
    • Gently stir the ingredients together with a cocktail stirrer or a long spoon
  9. Garnish:
    • If desired, garnish the Mojito with a lime wedge and a spring of fresh mint.
  10. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Your classic Mojito cocktail is now ready to be enjoyed. Sip and savor the refreshing minty
      and citrus flavors!
